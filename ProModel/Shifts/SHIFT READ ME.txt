Shift File Naming Convention

XX_YY_Z_ABBB

X= Starting time e.g 7a, 12p, etc.
Y= Ending time  e.g 7a, 12p, etc.
Z= Number of working days in the week
A= Number of breaks including lunch
B= Total number of minutes of break in a day (note: this includes lunch)


Example:

Shift definition:

Monday through Friday 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM,   There is a half hour lunch and 
2 fifteen minute breaks each day.

The name of the example shift file would be:

8a_5p_5_360
